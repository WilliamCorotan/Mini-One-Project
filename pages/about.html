History of DIEOS

DIEOS was started in 2018 by experienced professionals. On a small scale, DIEOS was built up in Davao Oriental. With a few years of experience in event organizing, our company rests on a secure base of solid expertise in the industry.

Vision

We are a wedding/event organizer in the Province of Davao Oriental that offers end-to-end (FULL) organizing service to our clientele from conceptualization, time and budget management to on the day coordination.

Mission

Every great event starts with organized details while executing them brilliantly!

DIEOS offers a variety of options to assist in making your occasion as perfect as you have imagined it! Whether it full venue decoration, full wedding planning, catering services or consulting that you need, we customize our packages to your needs.
