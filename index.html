Company Mission

As we have done numerous local and destination events as well as big bridal fairs, event planning is indeed our cup of tea. From dreaming up a theme and color palette to recommending suppliers, booking them, meeting and liaising with them down to the most diminutive details, we take care of these steps while assuring every client that they will have a stress-free preparation and a refined celebration.

We are a wedding/event planning company in the Philippines that offers end-to-end (FULL) planning service to our clientele from conceptualization, vendor recommendation, time and budget management to on the day coordination.
